Line Lift

Sketch on paper. Paint with real watercolor — on your phone.

Line Lift lifts the linework from a photo of your paper sketch and lets you paint it with a living watercolor engine: wet washes, blooms, dry brush, real drying. Your originals stay yours — everything lives on your device and in your own iCloud.

Support

Questions, problems, ideas — email fredrikande@gmail.com and you will get an answer from the person who makes the app. You can also send feedback directly from inside Line Lift (the "…" menu).
