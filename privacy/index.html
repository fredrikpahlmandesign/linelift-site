Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Line Lift is a watercolor painting app made by Fredrik Påhlman. It is built to need as little of your data as possible: no account, no ads, no tracking across other apps or websites, and nothing about you is ever sold or shared for marketing.

Your artwork stays yours

Everything you paint, capture, or import — drawings, photos, references, time-lapses — is stored on your device. It is never uploaded to us.

iCloud backup. So that your work survives a lost or replaced device, Line Lift can keep your sketchbook in your own iCloud (iCloud Drive's app container). This is on by default when you are signed in to iCloud, and you can switch it off at any time from the canvas "…" menu — turning it off moves your work back to device-only storage and removes it from iCloud. Your artwork syncs directly between your devices and Apple's iCloud under Apple's privacy terms; it never passes through our servers, and we have no access to it.

Camera and photo library

The camera is used only to capture your drawings so their linework can be lifted from the paper, and photo-library access happens only through Apple's system picker and share sheet — the app can see only the single photo you pick, and saves an image only when you choose Save. Images are processed on your device.

Anonymous usage analytics

To understand which parts of the app are used and where people get stuck, Line Lift sends a small, fixed set of anonymous usage events to PostHog, our analytics provider, hosted in the European Union (PostHog EU Cloud). The full list: app opened, capture started, capture completed, review opened, cleanup used, coloring started, share used, "capture next" selected, feedback opened, and feedback submitted. Each event carries only counts and coarse categories — things like how many pieces are in your gallery, whether a capture came from the camera or an import, how many seconds a capture took, a rough lift-quality tier (good / borderline / poor), whether a share was an image or a time-lapse — plus the app version, the number of days since first install, and a session counter. Never your name, your artwork, your photos, or anything that identifies you.

The app mints one random identifier on first launch so events from the same install can be counted together; it is not linked to you, your device serial, or any other app, and deleting the app deletes it. Events queue on your device and upload in small batches; if you are offline they simply wait.

In-app feedback

If you send feedback from inside the app, the text you type is transmitted to us (via the same EU analytics pipeline), together with which part of the app you were in, and an optional small screenshot of your current screen — the screenshot only if you switch its toggle on. Sending feedback is entirely voluntary, and it is the only way anything you have written or drawn can reach us.

What we never do

We do not collect names, emails, contacts, or location. We do not track you across apps or websites, show ads, sell data, or use third-party marketing SDKs.

Data retention and your rights

Anonymous analytics are retained in aggregate for product-improvement purposes. Because events are anonymous, we usually cannot connect them to a specific person; if you have sent feedback and would like it deleted, or have any question about this policy — including rights under the GDPR such as access, correction, and erasure — contact us and we will help: fredrikande@gmail.com.

Children

Line Lift is suitable for all ages and collects the same minimal, anonymous data from everyone.

Changes

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated and the effective date revised before the change takes effect. (14 July 2026: described iCloud backup, which is now part of the app, and listed the exact analytics events.)
